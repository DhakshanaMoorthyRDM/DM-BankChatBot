DM Bank chatbot

Chatbot - BUDDY This chatbot, named BUDDY, leverages advanced machine learning techniques to provide informative answers to a wide range of bank-related inquiries. Built using a custom dataset of customer questions and responses, BUDDY is fine-tuned with deep learning models (powered by PyTorch) to understand the nuances of natural language. By employing NLP methods like stemming, tokenization, and lemmatization, BUDDY can effectively process and interpret customer queries, resulting in an exceptional accuracy rate in addressing their banking needs.

We can usually notice that customers are more comfortable with chatbots as they are more robust and efficient when responding to queries than human agents. Chatbots are available 24/7, so customers can get help whenever they need it, without having to wait on hold or visit a branch during business hours. Additionally, chatbots can be programmed to answer a wide range of questions, so customers can get the information they need quickly and easily. For example, the Chatbot - BUDDY at DM bank is able to answer the customer's basic questions regarding the different types of accounts or the other banking products available.
